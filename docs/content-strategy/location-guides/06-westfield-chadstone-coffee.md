## Why Westfield Chadstone Coffee Matters

Westfield Chadstone's scale and prestige mean event coffee service must meet elevated standards. Unlike neighborhood events where mobile carts simply arrive and set up, shopping centre events require:

- **Pre-approved vendor status** (many venues have preferred vendor lists)
- **Public liability insurance** (mandatory for retail environments)
- **Understanding of shopping centre logistics** (loading docks, service corridors, setup timing)
- **Professional presentation** (Westfield's brand standards expect polished, on-brand service)
- **High-volume capacity** (shopping centre foot traffic creates large event attendance)

Vendors experienced with Westfield Chadstone navigate these requirements seamlessly, while newcomers may struggle with access and compliance.

## Westfield Chadstone Event Coffee Logistics

### Vendor Access Requirements

**Pre-Event Approval Process:**
1. **Vendor Registration:** Most external vendors need centre management approval
2. **Insurance Documentation:** Public liability insurance required
3. **Loading Dock Access:** Coordinate delivery timing
4. **Setup Timing:** Usually before centre opening (before 9am) or after hours

### Event Space Considerations

**Common Event Locations:**
- Public atrium areas (high foot traffic)
- Private retail spaces (store launches)
- Centre court/event spaces (large activations)
- Food court area (coordinate with Coffee Culture)

### Shopping Centre Brand Standards

Westfield Chadstone maintains high aesthetic standards:
- Cart appearance must be professional and well-maintained
- Baristas in branded uniforms
- Quality serviceware
- Clear waste management plan
