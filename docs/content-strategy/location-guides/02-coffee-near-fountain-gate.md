## What to Ask When Booking

1. **Do you service my specific suburb/venue?** Confirm coverage area and travel fees.
2. **What's your capacity for my guest count?** Ensure they can handle your expected numbers.
3. **Are weekends/peak dates available?** Confirm your event date ASAP.
4. **What's included in your base rate?** Clarify what's covered vs. add-on costs.
5. **Do you need power access?** Some carts have generators, others need venue power.
6. **What's your cancellation policy?** Understand terms if weather or plans change.
