# Getting Started with Coffee Carts in Melbourne

Melbourne's coffee culture is world-renowned, and bringing that experience to your event is easier than ever with mobile coffee carts.

## Why Choose a Coffee Cart?

- **Professional baristas** bring cafe-quality coffee to your venue
- **No setup required** - the cart arrives ready to serve
- **Perfect for events** of 20-200 guests
- **Flexible pricing** from $150-300/hour

## Popular Event Types

Coffee carts are perfect for:

1. Corporate events and conferences
2. Weddings and engagements
3. Market stalls and festivals
4. Private parties
